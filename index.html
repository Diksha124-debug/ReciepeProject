Easy Blackberry Cobbler

Ingredients

  • Blackbarries
  • Lemon juice
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Mint

Recipe

  1. Mix up the topping: Crack an egg into a medium bowl. Use a fork to break up the yolk, stirring until the yolk and white are combined.
  2. Build the cobbler: Using clean hands, sprinkle the topping mixture evenly
  3. Bake until golden and bubbly: Pop the dish into the oven.
